Why Most “Free” PDF Editors Are Not Really Free

Before we dive into ToolBox India's features, let us understand the problem with existing tools:

  • Watermarks on output: Many tools (Sejda, PDF Buddy) add visible watermarks to your edited PDF unless you pay
  • Daily file limits: iLovePDF limits free users to a small number of tasks per day. SmallPDF limits to 2 tasks/day
  • Forced signup: Adobe, DocHub, and many others require account creation before you can do anything
  • File upload to servers: Almost all online PDF tools upload your files to their cloud servers for processing — a serious privacy concern for sensitive documents like Aadhaar, PAN, bank statements, or legal contracts
  • Feature gating: Basic features are free but useful ones like compression, conversion, or signing require payment

The ToolBox India difference: Your PDF files are processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. They are never uploaded to any server. You can verify this by disconnecting from the internet after loading the page — the tools still work perfectly.

The Privacy Advantage: Why Browser-Based Matters

This is the single most important differentiator. When you use iLovePDF, SmallPDF, or any cloud-based PDF tool, your files are uploaded to their servers for processing. This means:

  • Your Aadhaar card, PAN card, bank statements, salary slips, contracts are sitting on someone else's server
  • The company's privacy policy may allow them to retain your files for a period (SmallPDF retains files for 1 hour; iLovePDF for 2 hours)
  • Even a brief retention window creates risk in case of a data breach
  • If you are working with NDA-covered documents or client files, uploading to third-party servers may violate your agreements

ToolBox India eliminates this risk entirely. Every operation runs in your browser's JavaScript engine. Your files exist only in your device's memory while you are working with them. When you close the tab, they are gone. There is no server, no database, no file storage — not even the capability to store your files, even if someone wanted to.

Verify it yourself: Open any ToolBox India PDF tool, load a file, then turn off your WiFi or mobile data. The tool keeps working. That is proof that your files never leave your device.

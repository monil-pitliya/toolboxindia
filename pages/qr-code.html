Frequently Asked Questions

Can I generate a UPI QR code?

Yes! Enter your UPI ID (e.g., name@upi, phone@paytm, or name@okaxis), display name, and optionally a fixed payment amount and note. The generated QR code works with all UPI apps including Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, and any other UPI-compatible payment app in India. Perfect for shops, restaurants, and freelancers to accept digital payments.

Can I add my logo to the QR code?

Yes! Upload any image — your brand logo, photo, or icon — and it will be centered inside the QR code. The high error correction level ensures the QR code remains fully scannable even with the logo overlay. This is ideal for creating branded QR codes for your business, marketing materials, and professional use.

Can I scan a QR code from a screenshot?

Yes! Upload any image (screenshot, photo, saved QR code) and the built-in scanner will detect and decode the QR code instantly. You can also use your device camera for live scanning in real time. This works with any QR code format — URLs, UPI, WiFi, vCard, and text-based QR codes.

Do the QR codes expire?

No, QR codes generated by Free Toolbox are permanent and never expire. Since the data is encoded directly into the QR pattern itself, they will work forever — there is no server or subscription involved. Unlike dynamic QR codes from some services that stop working when you cancel your subscription, our static QR codes are yours to keep for life.

Can I use this for my business?

Yes, this is perfect for businesses of all sizes. Generate UPI payment QR codes for your shop or restaurant, create custom branded QR codes with your company logo for marketing materials, share WiFi QR codes for your cafe or office, and create vCard QR codes for your business cards. All QR codes are free for commercial use with no attribution required.

How to create UPI QR code for my shop?

Select "UPI Payment" as the QR type, enter your UPI ID (e.g., yourname@upi or 9876543210@paytm), your shop or display name, and optionally set a fixed payment amount. Click generate, then download the QR code as a high-resolution PNG or SVG. Print it on a card or standee and display it at your billing counter. Customers can scan it with any UPI app — Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, or BHIM — to pay instantly. No POS machine required.

What dot styles are available?

Choose from multiple dot styles including square (the classic look), rounded (soft edges), dots (circular), classy (distinctive corners), classy-rounded (a premium hybrid), and extra-rounded (very smooth). You can also customize corner squares and corner dots separately to create a unique, branded appearance that matches your business identity.

Can I generate WiFi QR for my cafe?

Yes! Select "WiFi" as the QR type, enter your network name (SSID), password, and encryption type (WPA/WPA2 is most common). The generated QR code, when scanned by a guest's phone, automatically connects them to your WiFi without typing any password. This is extremely popular in cafes, restaurants, hotels, Airbnbs, co-working spaces, and offices across India. Print it on table cards or display it at the entrance.

Is the QR code high resolution for printing?

Yes! You can download your QR code in two formats: PNG for high-resolution raster images ideal for digital use, social media, and standard printing; or SVG for infinitely scalable vector graphics perfect for large banners, flex prints, hoardings, and professional marketing materials. SVG files maintain crisp quality at any print size — from a small business card to a large billboard.

Can I track QR code scans?

Free Toolbox generates static QR codes that encode data directly into the pattern, so built-in scan tracking is not available. However, you can easily add tracking by using a URL shortener with analytics (like Bitly or Google Campaign URLs with UTM parameters) as the QR content. Each scan that opens the URL will be recorded in the shortener's analytics dashboard, giving you scan counts, locations, devices, and more.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I select which pages to extract?

After uploading your PDF, the tool shows thumbnail previews of every page. Simply click on the pages you want to extract — selected pages are visually highlighted. You can also use Select All to grab every page, or Invert Selection to flip your current selection. This visual approach makes it easy to pick exactly the right pages.

What is the difference between Extract Pages and Split PDF?

Extract Pages creates a single new PDF containing only your selected pages — ideal when you want one file with specific pages pulled out. Split PDF divides a document into multiple separate PDF files based on page ranges. Use Extract when you want one combined result; use Split when you want several separate files.

Does extracting pages affect the original PDF?

No. The original PDF remains completely unchanged. Extracting pages creates a brand new PDF file containing only the pages you selected. Your original document stays exactly as it was — no pages removed, no content modified.

Can I preview pages before extracting?

Yes. The tool generates visual thumbnail previews of every page in your PDF. You can see exactly what each page contains before deciding which ones to include in the extracted document. This is especially useful for large PDFs where page numbers alone are not enough to identify content.

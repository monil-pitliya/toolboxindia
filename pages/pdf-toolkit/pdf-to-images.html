Frequently Asked Questions

Should I choose JPG or PNG format?

Choose JPG for photos and general use — it produces smaller file sizes with good visual quality, ideal for sharing on WhatsApp, Instagram, or email. Choose PNG for text-heavy PDFs, diagrams, screenshots, or when you need transparent backgrounds and pixel-perfect sharpness. PNG files are larger but completely lossless.

What are the 3 quality levels?

Standard quality is optimized for quick previews and sharing on WhatsApp, social media, or messaging apps. High quality is ideal for presentations, documents, and general professional use. Maximum quality produces the sharpest, highest-resolution images suitable for printing and archival purposes.

Can I convert specific pages instead of the entire PDF?

Yes. You can select which pages to convert instead of processing the entire document. This is especially useful when you only need images of certain pages from a large PDF — saving both time and storage space on your device.

Can I download all images as a ZIP file?

Yes. After conversion, you can download each image individually or download all converted images together as a single ZIP archive. The ZIP option is much more convenient when converting multi-page PDFs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Upload your PDF to the Rotate PDF tool, then click the rotate button on any page thumbnail. Each click rotates that page 90 degrees clockwise. Click once for 90 degrees, twice for 180 degrees, or three times for 270 degrees. You can also use the batch rotate option to rotate all pages at once.

Can I rotate just one page in a multi-page PDF?

Absolutely. The tool displays every page as a visual thumbnail. Click the rotate button on any individual page to rotate just that page while leaving all other pages unchanged. This is ideal for fixing scanned documents where some pages were fed sideways into the scanner.

Is this rotate PDF tool free and private?

Yes, completely free with no signup required, and 100% private. Your PDF is processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. The file is never uploaded to any server. No data leaves your device, ever. There are no watermarks added to your output.

Can I rotate a PDF on my phone?

Yes. The Rotate PDF tool is fully responsive and works on mobile phones, tablets, and desktops. The thumbnail preview adapts to your screen size so you can rotate pages comfortably on any device, including Android and iOS.

What is the maximum file size for rotating a PDF?

There is no server-side limit because everything runs in your browser. The practical limit depends on your device's available memory. Most modern devices can handle PDFs up to 100MB or more without issues. For very large files, ensure you have sufficient free RAM.

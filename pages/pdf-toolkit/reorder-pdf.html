Frequently Asked Questions

How do I rearrange pages in a PDF?

Upload your PDF to the Reorder PDF tool on Free Toolbox. Every page appears as a visual thumbnail. Simply drag any page thumbnail and drop it at the position where you want it. You can rearrange as many pages as you need. When you are satisfied with the new order, click Download to save the reordered PDF.

Can I move the last page to the front of the PDF?

Yes, you can move any page to any position in the document. Drag the last page thumbnail and drop it at the very beginning of the grid. The tool supports moving pages across any distance — first to last, last to first, or anywhere in between. There are no restrictions.

Does reordering change the content of my PDF?

No. Reordering only changes the sequence of pages. All content, formatting, images, links, annotations, and text on each page remain exactly the same. Only the page order changes in the output file. No quality is lost.

Is there a page limit for reordering?

No artificial limit. The tool runs entirely in your browser, so it can handle as many pages as your device memory allows. Documents with hundreds of pages work smoothly on modern devices with sufficient RAM.

Can I reorder PDF pages on my mobile phone?

Yes. The drag-and-drop interface is touch-friendly and works on mobile phones and tablets running any operating system. Tap and hold a page thumbnail, then drag it to the desired position. The responsive layout adapts to all screen sizes for a smooth experience.

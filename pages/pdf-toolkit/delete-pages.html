Frequently Asked Questions

How do I select which pages to delete?

After uploading your PDF, the tool displays thumbnail previews of every page. Click on any page you want to remove — it will be highlighted in red to indicate it is marked for deletion. Click again on a marked page to unmark it if you change your mind. This visual approach ensures you always know exactly which pages will be removed.

What happens if I try to delete all pages?

The tool includes smart validation checks to prevent you from deleting every page. A valid PDF must contain at least one page, so if your selection would result in an empty document, the tool displays a warning and prevents the operation.

Does deleting pages modify my original PDF?

No. Deleting pages creates a brand new PDF without the removed pages. Your original file remains completely unchanged on your device. The tool never modifies, overwrites, or alters your source document in any way.

Can I preview pages before deleting them?

Yes. The tool generates visual thumbnail previews of every page in your PDF. You can inspect each page before marking it for deletion, ensuring you only remove the pages you truly do not need. This is especially useful for large documents where page numbers alone are not enough.

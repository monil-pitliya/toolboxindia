Frequently Asked Questions

Can I compress a PDF to exactly 200KB?

Yes. Select the 200KB preset in Target Size mode. The tool iteratively adjusts compression to get as close to 200KB as possible. This is especially useful for Indian government form submissions, UPSC applications, SSC exam forms, and job portals that require PDFs under a specific size limit.

Does compression make the text blurry or non-selectable?

No. Smart Compression mode keeps all text selectable, sharp, and searchable. The tool intelligently compresses images within the PDF while preserving text quality. Even in Target Size mode, text remains fully readable and selectable — this is not a screenshot-based compressor.

What target sizes are available?

Preset target sizes include 200KB, 500KB, 1MB, 2MB, 5MB, and 10MB. You can also enter any custom target size in KB or MB. The tool automatically adjusts compression to match your target as closely as possible.

Can I compress a PDF for email attachment?

Absolutely. Most email providers (Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo) have a 25MB attachment limit. Use the Target Size mode to compress your PDF to any size that fits.

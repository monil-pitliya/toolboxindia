Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Text Only and Text + Images mode?

Text Only mode extracts just the text content from your PDF, producing a clean, lightweight Word document that is fast to generate and small in file size. Text + Images mode also includes images from the PDF embedded in the Word file. Choose Text Only when you just need to edit the text, or Text + Images when you need the full visual content preserved.

Can I edit the converted Word file?

Yes. The output is a standard .docx file that you can open and edit in Microsoft Word, Google Docs, LibreOffice Writer, WPS Office, or any other word processor that supports the DOCX format. All text is fully editable.

Does the converter preserve formatting?

The tool extracts text and images from the PDF and creates a well-formatted Word document organized by page sections. While highly complex layouts with multiple columns may differ slightly, the text content, font sizes, and images are preserved accurately for everyday editing needs.

Is my PDF uploaded to any server?

No. Free Toolbox's PDF to Word converter runs 100% in your browser using JavaScript. Your file never leaves your device — there are no servers, no uploads, no tracking. This makes it completely safe for converting confidential documents like Aadhaar cards, PAN cards, bank statements, and legal documents.

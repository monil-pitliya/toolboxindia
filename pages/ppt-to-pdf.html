Frequently Asked Questions

Does it work with .ppt files?

The converter works best with .pptx files (PowerPoint 2007 and later format). If you have an older .ppt file (PowerPoint 97-2003), save it as .pptx first in Microsoft PowerPoint, LibreOffice Impress, or Google Slides. Open your .ppt file, use "Save As" and select .pptx format, then upload the converted file here. Most modern presentations are already in .pptx format.

Are animations preserved in the PDF?

Static content is fully preserved in the PDF output. Animations and transitions become their final visible frame — all elements on a slide are captured in their end state, but motion effects are not applicable in PDF format since PDFs are static documents. This means every text box, image, and shape appears in the PDF exactly as it looks when the slide animation is complete.

Can I convert Google Slides to PDF?

Yes! First download your presentation as .pptx from Google Slides (File > Download > Microsoft PowerPoint .pptx), then upload the .pptx file here for instant conversion to a high-quality PDF. Your presentation data stays on your device with ToolBox India.

Does it preserve slide animations?

PDF is a static document format, so slide animations (fly-in, fade, zoom, appear effects) and slide transitions (morph, push, wipe, dissolve) cannot be replicated in PDF. However, all animated elements appear in their final visible state in the PDF. If you have content that appears sequentially through click animations, all elements will be visible together on the same page in the PDF output. For best results, review the preview before downloading.

Indian Passport Photo Specifications

  • Size — 2x2 inches (51x51mm) or 35x45mm
  • Background — Plain white, no patterns or shadows
  • Face coverage — Face should cover 70-80% of the frame
  • Expression — Neutral expression, mouth closed, eyes open
  • Glasses — No glasses unless medically prescribed (no glare)
  • Ears — Both ears should be visible
  • Head covering — Only for religious reasons; face must be fully visible from chin to forehead
  • Recency — Photo should be taken within the last 6 months

Frequently Asked Questions

What background color is required for Indian passport photos?

Indian passport photos require a plain white background as per the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) guidelines. The background must be uniformly white without any patterns, shadows, or other objects. ToolBox India's Background Remover replaces any background with pure white (#FFFFFF) that meets this requirement.

What are the Indian passport photo specifications?

Indian passport photo specs: 2x2 inches (51x51mm) or 35x45mm, white background, face covering 70-80% of frame, front-facing with neutral expression, no glasses (unless medically prescribed), both ears visible, photo taken within the last 6 months. File size limits vary by portal.

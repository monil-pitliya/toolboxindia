Frequently Asked Questions

Does the UPI QR code work with all UPI apps?

Yes! The generated QR code uses the standard UPI protocol (upi://pay) defined by NPCI, which is compatible with all UPI apps including Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, Amazon Pay, WhatsApp Pay, CRED, and every other UPI-enabled banking app in India.

Can I set a fixed payment amount in the QR code?

Yes! You can set an optional fixed amount. When someone scans the QR code, the amount will be pre-filled in their UPI app. They can still modify the amount before confirming payment. This is useful for fixed-price items, subscription payments, or course fees.

How do I create a Google Pay QR code?

Open ToolBox India's QR Code Generator, select UPI Payment type, enter your UPI ID (the one linked to Google Pay), add your name, and generate. The QR code works with Google Pay and all other UPI apps — there is no separate "Google Pay QR code" since UPI is a universal standard defined by NPCI.

Can I add my shop logo to the QR code?

Yes! Upload your shop logo, brand icon, or any image and it will appear centered inside the QR code. The high error correction level ensures the QR code remains scannable even with the logo overlay. This makes your payment QR look professional.

Is it safe to display my UPI QR code publicly?

Yes, displaying a UPI QR code is completely safe. It only contains your UPI ID and optional name/amount. People can only send you money by scanning it — they cannot debit from your account or access your bank details. Millions of shops across India display UPI QR codes publicly.

What UPI ID format should I use?

Use the UPI ID from your preferred UPI app. Common formats: name@upi (BHIM), name@okaxis or name@okhdfcbank (Google Pay), name@ybl (PhonePe), name@paytm (Paytm). Find your UPI ID in your app's profile or settings section.

Can I create UPI QR codes for my restaurant or shop?

Absolutely! Generate a QR code with your business UPI ID, add your shop or restaurant logo, download the high-resolution PNG or SVG, and print it as a counter standee, table tent, or wall sticker. Customers scan and pay instantly from any UPI app.

Do these QR codes expire?

No! UPI QR codes generated by ToolBox India are permanent and never expire. The QR code encodes your UPI ID, so as long as your UPI ID is active with your bank, the QR code will work indefinitely. No subscription or renewal needed.

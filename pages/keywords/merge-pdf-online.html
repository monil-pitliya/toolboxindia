No! ToolBox India processes everything in your browser using JavaScript and WebAssembly. Your PDFs are never uploaded to any server. This is a major privacy advantage over iLovePDF, SmallPDF, and Adobe online tools that upload your files to their cloud servers.

Can I merge password-protected PDFs?

If the PDF requires a password to open (encrypted), you will need to enter the password first. If the PDF opens normally but has restrictions on editing/printing, the merge tool can typically process it without issues.

Will merging affect PDF quality?

No! Merging PDFs is a lossless operation. The tool combines PDF pages as-is without re-encoding or compressing any content. All text, images, formatting, fonts, and hyperlinks are preserved exactly as they were in the original files.

Can I rearrange pages before merging?

Does it work on mobile phones?

Yes! The PDF merger works perfectly on all smartphones and tablets including budget Android phones. Upload PDFs from your phone storage, Google Drive, or any file manager app. The merged PDF saves directly to your phone's Downloads folder.

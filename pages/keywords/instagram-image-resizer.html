Why Image Size Matters on Instagram

  • Avoid unwanted cropping — Instagram crops images that do not match supported ratios. Pre-sizing prevents losing important parts of your photo
  • Maximum quality — Instagram downscales images wider than 1080px and upscales images smaller than 320px (causing blur). Upload at exactly 1080px wide for best quality
  • More feed space — Portrait posts (4:5) take up 25% more screen space than square posts, leading to higher engagement and more time on screen
  • Professional look — Correctly sized images look crisp and professional, while incorrectly sized ones appear blurry or awkwardly cropped
  • Consistent carousel — All carousel slides must match the first slide's aspect ratio. Pre-sizing ensures consistency across all slides
  • Faster loading — Right-sized images load faster on followers' feeds, especially important for Indian users on mobile data

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best image size for Instagram posts?

The best image size for Instagram posts is 1080x1080 pixels (1:1 square) for the classic look, or 1080x1350 pixels (4:5 portrait) for maximum feed presence and engagement. Portrait posts take up more screen space and typically get better engagement metrics.

What are Instagram story dimensions?

Instagram stories should be 1080x1920 pixels (9:16 ratio). This is the full-screen vertical format that fills the entire phone screen. The same dimensions apply to Instagram Reel cover images and IGTV covers.

What size is an Instagram profile picture?

Instagram profile pictures display at 110x110 pixels on mobile and 180x180 pixels on desktop. However, upload at 320x320 pixels minimum for best quality across all devices. Instagram crops it to a circle, so keep the subject centered.

What is the Instagram carousel size?

Instagram carousel posts support three ratios: 1:1 square (1080x1080), 4:5 portrait (1080x1350), and 1.91:1 landscape (1080x566). All slides in a carousel must share the same aspect ratio. The first image determines the ratio for all subsequent slides.

Will Instagram crop my image?

Instagram crops images that do not match supported aspect ratios (1:1, 4:5, or 1.91:1). By resizing your image to the exact dimensions using ToolBox India before uploading, you avoid unwanted cropping and maintain full control over your composition.

What resolution does Instagram support?

Instagram supports images between 320px and 1080px wide. Images wider than 1080px are automatically downscaled. For best quality, always upload at 1080px width. Images smaller than 320px are upscaled by Instagram, which causes visible blur.

How do I resize for Instagram Reels?

Instagram Reels are vertical videos at 1080x1920 pixels (9:16 ratio). For Reel cover/thumbnail images, use the same 1080x1920 dimensions. ToolBox India's Image Resizer has a dedicated preset for this exact size.

Does resizing affect image quality?

Resizing to a smaller dimension slightly reduces detail, but at 1080px width the quality remains excellent for Instagram display. Upscaling (making smaller images larger) causes blur. For best results, always start with a high-resolution original photo from your phone camera.

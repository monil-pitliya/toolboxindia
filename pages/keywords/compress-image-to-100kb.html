UPSC and SSC Photo Upload Guide

For UPSC Civil Services Examination, your photo should be a recent passport-size color photograph with a white background. The file must be in JPG/JPEG format, between 20KB and 300KB. Use our compressor to resize your scanned photo to fit within these limits while keeping facial features clear.

For SSC exams (CGL, CHSL, MTS, GD), photo requirements are stricter, typically 20-50KB in JPG format. Signature scans must be 10-20KB. Our tool's target size feature makes this effortless -- just enter the target KB and download.

Quality Slider Tips

80-100% Quality: Minimal compression, best for when your image is already close to the target size. Produces the sharpest output.

60-80% Quality: Good balance of size reduction and clarity. Recommended for most government form photos. Text in the image remains readable.

40-60% Quality: Significant size reduction. Suitable for small photo thumbnails and web use. Fine for passport-size images where extreme detail is not critical.

Below 40%: Maximum compression. Use only when you need very small file sizes (under 20KB). Some visual artifacts may be visible.

Why do government portals require PDFs under 200KB?

Indian government portals like DigiLocker, EPFO, income tax e-filing, and various state portals set file size limits (100KB-500KB) to handle millions of simultaneous submissions efficiently and reduce server storage costs.

Can I compress a scanned PDF to 200KB?

Yes, but scanned PDFs are essentially images inside a PDF wrapper, so they start much larger (often 2-10MB). Use high compression to reduce image quality. For best results, scan at 150-200 DPI instead of 300 DPI when creating the original scan.

For text-based PDFs, compression mainly removes metadata, optimizes fonts, and reduces embedded image quality. The text itself remains sharp and fully readable. Only embedded photographs or charts may show slight quality reduction.
